Sam Iam

Hey! Thanks for visiting my blog. I have loved birds for as long as I can remember. My mom was an avid birder and frequently took me along with her. I got my first pair of binoculars at the age of six and haven't left the house without them since. If I had to choose, I would say that my favorite bird is the Stellar Jay. The blue accented shimmer of it's body stands in stark contrast to it's black head.
