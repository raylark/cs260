Easy to work with
Ronald McDonald

Jans was really easy to work with. I came with an idea of what angles and lighting I wanted for the shoot. Jans proceeded to rip up the paper I had written them on and said, "As if!" I was grateful that my vision didn't get in the way of me purchasing predone photos. The best part was that I got to contribute even more money to Jans company as the photos came bundle with some paracord keychains. Best $300 I've ever spent, thank you Frogtography!
